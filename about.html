About Oxford Sofa

Welcome to Oxford Sofa — a symbol of refined comfort and timeless craftsmanship. At Oxford Sofa, we believe luxury is not just seen — it’s experienced. Every piece we create is thoughtfully designed to elevate your living space with elegance, sophistication, and enduring quality. From meticulously crafted pre-made collections to fully customized, order-made designs, we offer furniture that complements your lifestyle with effortless grace.

Our Story

Oxford Sofa began with a simple vision: to redefine the meaning of premium furniture. What started as a boutique workshop dedicated to exceptional craftsmanship has evolved into a distinguished brand trusted for its artistry, durability, and signature aesthetic. Today, we blend classic design principles with modern innovation to craft pieces that stand the test of time.

What We Offer

Pre-Made Luxury Collections

Discover our curated selection of ready-made sofas and furnishings — designed with precision, built with the finest materials, and finished to perfection.

Order-Made Furniture
